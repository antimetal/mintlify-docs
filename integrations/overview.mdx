Connect Antimetal to the platforms your team already relies on. Antimetal pulls data from your cloud providers, monitoring tools, and source control to investigate, fix, and prevent production issues.

## How Integrations Work

All integrations are set up in the [Antimetal dashboard](https://overlook.antimetal.com/integrations). Some integrations require additional configuration in the third-party tool. Those have dedicated setup guides linked below.
